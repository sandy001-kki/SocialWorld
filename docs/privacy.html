SocialWorld — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

When you log in with Facebook, we collect your public profile name and profile picture only. We do not collect email addresses, friend lists, or any private data.

2. How We Use It

Your name and profile picture are used solely to create your building in the SocialWorld 3D city. Your building is visible to all visitors of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your profile data is stored securely in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

4. Facebook Login

We use Facebook Login only to identify you. We request the public_profile permission only. We do not post on your behalf or access your messages.

5. Cookies

We use a single session token (stored in localStorage) to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies are used.

6. Data Deletion

To remove your data from SocialWorld, contact us at bollavaramsandeep@gmail.com and we will delete your record within 7 days.
